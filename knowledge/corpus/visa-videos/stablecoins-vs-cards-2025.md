# Will stablecoins and traditional payments compete or work together? (2025)
Source: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pfNOBw_osxY

I think that's a definitive work together. Cryptocurrency of all forms, including stablecoins and traditional payment methods, already work together. We at Visa facilitate the onboarding of fiat currency into stablecoins and the offboarding of stablecoins and cryptocurrency into fiat currency in enormous quantities today. And we've been doing that for many years. That continues to grow with each passing day.
