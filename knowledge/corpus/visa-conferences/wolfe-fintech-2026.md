# Wolfe FinTech Forum 2026 — Jack Forestell

Source: https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/visa-at-wolfe-fintech-forum-strategic-insights-on-agentic-commerce-93CH-4555086
Date: March 2026
Format: investing.com transcript / executive summary

## Key Topics & Quotes

### Agentic Commerce as Growth Opportunity
Forestell positioned agentic commerce as transformational:

> "I have not stared into a bigger growth opportunity than what we have ahead of us in the development of the agentic web."

He compared it to e-commerce's emergence, noting agents will increase transaction success rates and payment density.

### Consumer Health & Economic Resilience
On current conditions:

> "The watch words for the consumer driving out of the data that I'm seeing are stability and resilience."

He rejected the K-shaped economy narrative, describing economic growth patterns as more "E-shaped" — consistent positive growth across income levels.

### Stablecoin Momentum
Settlement volume in stablecoins reached **$4.5+ billion in annualized run rate** in the last quarter — a dramatic increase from near-zero a year prior. Forestell emphasized stablecoins improve backend settlement infrastructure while Visa serves as a bridge to existing front-end payment systems.

### AI Integration in Products
Visa uses AI to screen "hundreds of parameters in sub-second time" for fraud detection. He highlighted product development acceleration through AI coding tools, with one team completing prototype-to-documentation cycles in days rather than months.

### Standards Development
Forestell stressed:

> "These standards need to exist to make the two machine readable sides work."

Visa published its Intelligent Commerce specifications and Trusted Agent Protocol to enable agent-merchant interactions.
