# Oliver Jenkyn — Profile

## Current role

**Group President, Global Markets** at Visa Inc.

Responsible for Visa's business in **200+ countries and territories** worldwide. The Global Markets organization includes:
- Global FX and Cross Border Solutions (a major P&L line)
- Country-level GMs across all regions (NA, LatAm, Europe, CEMEA, AP)
- A senior executive structure with multiple SVPs and VPs reporting up

## Career arc at Visa

(in order, sourced from public Visa press releases and his Visa IR bio)

1. **Global Head of Strategy and Corporate Development** — corporate strategy + M&A
2. **President, North America** — running Visa's largest single-country business
3. **Global head of new businesses** — covered:
   - Commercial / B2B
   - Government solutions
   - Visa Direct (real-time push payments)
4. **Group President, Global Markets** (current) — running the network's geographic footprint

## Pre-Visa background

**McKinsey & Company** — strategy consulting. (15+ years at Visa as of 2026.)
